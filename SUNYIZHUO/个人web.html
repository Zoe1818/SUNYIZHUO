关于HKBU

Hong Kong Baptist College is a private institution of higher learning founded by the Hong Kong Baptist Convention in 1956. It is committed to providing holistic education. In 1983, the then Hong Kong Baptist College received full government funding and became a public higher education institution. In 1994, it was renamed Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU).
